Octave Capital partners with venture-backed and founder run technology companies to provide flexible growth capital and liquidity.
Founded by Jeremy Giffon and based in New York, we invest $5–50m in businesses generating $5m+ in ARR, investing both primary capital for growth and secondary capital for founder liquidity/recapitalization. With a concentrated approach of 1–3 investments per year, we provide dedicated focus and resources to help founders build flourishing businesses.
Our team of software investors and operators specializes in solving the complex capital structure challenges that emerge as companies scale – creating alignment and optionality in a broad range of outcomes.
