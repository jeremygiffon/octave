Ledger

Privacy Policy

Last updated 28 May 2026

Ledger is a personal-use iPhone application used to track finances by connecting to a QuickBooks Online account. It is built and operated by Octave Capital for private, individual use. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

Information we collect

When you connect Ledger to QuickBooks Online, you authorize access through Intuit's OAuth sign-in flow. Ledger then reads financial data from your QuickBooks Online company, which may include:

  • Bank and account balances
  • Profit & loss, cash flow, and other financial reports
  • Transactions, vendors, and related accounting records
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by Intuit, and the associated company (realm) identifier

Ledger does not ask for, collect, or store any other personal information. There are no accounts to create, and the app does not use analytics or advertising trackers.

How we use your information

Financial data is used for one purpose only: to display and track personal finances inside the app, on your device, for you. It is not used for any other purpose, and it is never sold, rented, or used for advertising.

How your information is stored

OAuth tokens are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device. All communication with Intuit's QuickBooks Online API happens over encrypted HTTPS connections. Financial data is fetched on demand for display and is not retained on any third-party server operated by us.

Sharing your information

We do not share your data with any third parties. The only external service the app communicates with is Intuit / QuickBooks Online, which is the source of your data and is governed by Intuit's own privacy policy. We do not transmit your financial data to anyone else.

Revoking access

You can disconnect Ledger at any time from within the app, or by revoking the app's access from your Intuit account settings. Doing so invalidates the stored tokens and ends the app's access to your QuickBooks Online data.

Children's privacy

Ledger is not directed to children and is not intended for use by anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
